FAQ

How can NGOs help?
NGOs can help by sharing our resources with farmers, co-hosting field workshops, helping with registration drives, and translating or localizing content for better accessibility.
What support can we offer?
We provide co-branded materials, access to digital modules, guidance on using the platform, and can help set up pilot programs in targeted regions.
Is there a cost?
No — basic partnerships that focus on outreach, education, and content-sharing are free. For larger collaborations or funded pilots, we can discuss cost-sharing or sponsored programming.

Suggested workflow for handling responses

  1. Form submissions are saved to localStorage under the key ngoPartners (demo). For production, POST the data to a secure backend or use a form-service.
  2. Notify your admin inbox (we suggest replacing admin@example.com in the code with your real admin email) — or set up a server endpoint to receive submissions and store them in a database.
  3. Assign a partnerships coordinator to follow up within 3 business days and schedule a discovery call.
